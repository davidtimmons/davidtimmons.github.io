How To Create Compelling Social Media Marketing

March 21, 2013 7:00am
category ➞ Marketing

Photo: "SAAB B-engine" by Staffan Vilcans

"A soaring signal-to-noise ratio from your social media marketing creates a relevant brand."

The signal-to-noise ratio in social media marketing can be described as a comparison of value versus junk. Becoming relevant takes work. Your content, its context, and your conversation methods must all be in sync.

Begin tuning your social media marketing by following the timeless advice of Strunk & White: omit needless words!

Everything you communicate must strengthen your core idea.
